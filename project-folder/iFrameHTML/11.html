11. FINANCIAL AID SUSPENSION 

Result:  You will be on Financial Aid Suspension next quarter, so you will not qualify for aid. 

Detail: You will not be eligible for Financial Aid next quarter and you may have to repay funds disbursed to your account.  You may complete a Petition for Reinstatement of Aid Eligibility.  Please see a Financial Aid counselor to discuss your options and explore resources.
