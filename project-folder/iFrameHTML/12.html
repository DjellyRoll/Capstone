12. FINANCIAL AID WARNING 

Result:  You will be on Financial Aid Warning next quarter. 

Detail: You will still be eligible for Financial Aid next quarter, but it will be a Warning quarter for you.  If you meet all requirements going forward, then you improve the likelihood that you will continue to qualify for aid.  If next quarter you do not meet all the financial aid requirements, then you may not qualify for aid in the next quarter.  Please see a Financial Aid counselor to discuss your options and explore resources.
