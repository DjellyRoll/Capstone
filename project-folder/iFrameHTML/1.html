1. WHAT IF I WITHDRAW? 

Question: What will happen to my financial aid if I withdraw from one or more classes?   

How will it impact me today and next quarter?  This tool is designed to help you model answers to those questions based on the responses that you select.  Modeling your situation here will give you background information and prepare you to ask good questions.  Use this tool to understand your options and the outcomes that your choices will produce, and continue the conversation with the people in the financial aid office who can help you explore your options and resources more deeply.
