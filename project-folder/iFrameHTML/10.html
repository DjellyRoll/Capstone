PREVIOUS FINANCIAL AID WARNING

Question: Is this a Warning quarter for me?

You have reached this step because you didn't meet a requirement. If this is the first time that this is the case, then make plans to ensure that you will meet all requirements going forward. Any student who is already in a Warning quarter who doesn't meet all the requirements will be ineligible for aid next quarter. You can check to see if you are on Warning on Web4U.
