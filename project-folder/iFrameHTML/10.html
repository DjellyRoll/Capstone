10. PREVIOUS FINANCIAL AID WARNING 

Question: Is this a Warning quarter for me? 

Details: You have reached this step because you didn’t meet a requirement.  If this is the first time that this is the case, then make plans to ensure that you will meet all requirements going forward.  Any student who is already in a Warning quarter who doesn’t meet all the requirements will be ineligible for aid next quarter.  You can check to see if you are on Warning on Web4U. 

Next: 

If you are already on Warning this quarter, then go to step 11. 

If you are not already on Warning this quarter, then go to step 12.
