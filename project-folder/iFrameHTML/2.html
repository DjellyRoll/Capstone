Question: What is my Financial Aid status today?

Details: The primary purpose of financial aid programs is to help students successfully complete their degree or certificate program in a timely way. This tool can be used by anyone who has received financial aid, but if you are already on a special Financial Aid status, then you should speak to a Financial Aid counselor in person as soon as possible.
