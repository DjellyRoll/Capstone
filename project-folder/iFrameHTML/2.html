2. CURRENT FINANCIAL AID STATUS 

Question: What is my Financial Aid status today? 

Detail: The primary purpose of financial aid programs is to help students successfully complete their degree or certificate program in a timely way. Financial aid programs include grants, tuition and fee waivers, work study employment, need-based and non-need-based loans, and scholarship programs.  This tool can be used by anyone who has received financial aid, but if you are already on a special Financial Aid status, then you should speak to a Financial Aid counselor in person as soon as possible.  Go to Web4U to verify your current Financial Aid Status, and use that to choose the next step. 

If you have not received any Financial Aid, then you should determine how dropping a class will affect your academic status, not your Financial Aid Status.  Go to step 13. 

If you are on Financial Aid Warning, Suspension, Probation, or Probation with Plan, then go to step 15. 

Otherwise, go to step 3.
