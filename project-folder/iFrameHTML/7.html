7. REVISION FORM 

Requirement: To proceed, download and submit the revision form to the Financial Aid office. 

Details: You must submit the completed revision form to the financial aid office before the deadline in order to process the change.  Get the form here.  If today’s date is within the first five days of the quarter, then your deadline is the close of business on the fifth day.  If today’s date falls from the sixth day of the quarter to the second Friday of the quarter, then your deadline is the close of business on the second Friday. If you miss the deadline, then you enter a new deadline period. 

Next: 

If you submit the form to the financial aid office before the end of business on the day of the deadline, then go to step 8. 

If you do not submit the form to the financial aid office before the end of business on the day of the deadline, then go to step 14.
